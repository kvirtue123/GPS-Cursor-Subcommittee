GPS Cursor Subcommittee Hub
:dart: Purpose

* Learn the tools — stay current on Cursor and the broader AI coding tool landscape.
* Document best practices — capture what works, what doesn't, and why.
* Share that knowledge as broadly as possible — enable GPS SEs beyond this committee.


:sparkles: Group's Value-Add

What this committee produces for the wider GPS SE org:

* Tool use cases — how GPS SEs should be utilizing the toolsets
* GPS AI tooling demos — more to come shortly
* Lessons learned & recommendations — best practices grounded in what we're actually doing
* Guardrails — what to watch out for, how to prevent shortcuts we DON'T want our customers to take, and how to enable creativity safely


:white_check_mark: What We Are (and Aren't)

:heavy_check_mark: We Are

* Sharers of Cursor knowledge, practical success stories, and best practices
* "Workflow experimenters" figuring out when to use (and when not to use) Cursor
* A collaborative, living knowledge base that centralizes prompts and reusable assets to reduce duplicate work
* A group suggesting helpful guardrails for Cursor development

:x: We Are Not

* A technical support desk or customer support for the product
* A specialized "black belt" team of builders pulled in to support random deals
* Trying to replicate the formal, broad enablement content already created by the COE
* Core product developers or rigid policy enforcers
